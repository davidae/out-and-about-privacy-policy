Child Safety Policy for OutAndAbout

Effective Date: May 02, 2025

OutAndAbout is committed to protecting children and preventing any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform. This policy outlines our standards, safety practices, and reporting mechanisms to uphold child safety in line with Google Playâ€™s developer policies and global child protection expectations.

1. Our Commitment

We have zero tolerance for any content or behavior that involves child sexual abuse, exploitation, grooming, sextortion, or trafficking. We proactively work to prevent and respond to any such activities on our platform.

2. Safety Standards

  • No individuals under 18 are permitted to use the app.
  • User profiles and content are monitored for indicators of CSAE or grooming behavior.
  • Reporting tools are available in-app for users to flag inappropriate behavior or content.
  • Accounts involved in any CSAE behavior are permanently banned and reported to relevant authorities.

3. Detection and Moderation

We monitor activity using automated and manual tools to detect potential CSAE violations. This includes flagging inappropriate chat content, profile images, or suspicious behavior patterns.

4. Reporting and Escalation

  • Any CSAE-related reports are reviewed immediately.
  • Confirmed incidents are escalated to local law enforcement and appropriate child protection agencies (such as NCMEC, BKA, or Europol).
  • We cooperate fully with investigations and maintain records to support legal follow-ups.
